Leafy's Somewhat Interesting Interests

I enjoy many different hobbies but my favorites are cooking, crocheting and camping! Although camping is only in the spring/summer time, that's okay because that leaves plenty of time to work on other things. I also love to read and watch movies. Growing up my grandfather had hundreds of movies on VHS tapes, three to a tape! I remember having to rewind and fast forward to get to the movies we wanted to watch as kids.

In order to get to Hocus Pocus you'd have to fast forward through Huckleberry Finn. I think I saw that movie hundreds of times before actually watching it. When those fancy VHS rewinders came out it made life a lot easier. They didn't last that long though because soon enough my grandfather was on to collecting DVDs. He eventually donated the VHS collection to the nurisng home where my Mom Mom lived prior to her passing.

Breakfast

Breakfast food is truly my favorite food. I grew up in and out of the diners that my momma worked at and let me tell YOU. There is nothing better than walkin' into a diner and smellin' that aromatic coffee dripping and the breakfast sizzling on the griddle. Who ever has a bad time at a diner?! It's a damn shame they are falling out of favor around the country but I am awfully proud that southern NJ has succeeded in keeping them alive.

My favorite breakfast in the world is scrambled eggs, home fries with crispy fried onions, crispy bacon and some toast. Don't forget the coffee and OJ.

Soup

What's better than a hot and delicious soup on a cold autumn or winter's day? Add some homemade bread in there and you're good to go for all eternity. Mmmm, soup.... Sorry, anyway! My favorite soups are potato soup, chicken soup and beef stew. They are all super easy to make in my pressure cooker or on the stove. I prefer my Ninja Foodi because it's faster to make dinner when I get home from work.
